SUMMARY:
The EEAS report on Foreign Information Manipulation and Interference analyzes tactics, techniques, procedures, narratives, actor behavior, and response frameworks related to hostile information activity. It is relevant because a RAG assistant must distinguish content narratives from infrastructure, coordination, impersonation, amplification, and cross-platform behavior. The source helps classify FIMI incidents without treating every false claim as the same kind of operation. It pairs well with OECD communication principles and Microsoft threat reporting.
