SUMMARY:
CISA material on Sector Risk Management Agencies explains how U.S. critical-infrastructure sectors are coordinated across lead federal agencies and sector partners. It is relevant because RAG answers about infrastructure risk need to identify which sector is affected and who has coordination responsibility. The source helps classify energy, water, transportation, communications, health care, financial services, food and agriculture, and other sectors. It pairs with NIST and CISA cyber sources for governance-level reasoning.
